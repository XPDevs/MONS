Introduction

Welcome to the Monopoly Simulation Guide! This page will help you learn the best ways to play Monopoly, with insights from simulated outcomes.

Instructions

  1. Launch the Monopoly simulation application.
  2. Select the number of players and initial settings.
  3. Click "Start Simulation" to begin.
  4. Observe the outcomes and analyze strategies.

Strategies

Here are some recommended strategies based on simulation results:

  • Property Acquisition: Prioritize buying properties with high traffic and potential for building houses and hotels.
  • Railroads and Utilities: Invest in these early for consistent income.
  • Developing Properties: Build houses and hotels on monopolies to maximize rent income.
  • Trade Wisely: Make strategic trades to complete monopolies and improve your position.
